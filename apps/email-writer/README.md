# Email Writer

AI-powered email drafting assistant for VibeDepot.

## Features

- **Tone selection** — Choose from professional, casual, friendly, formal, or apologetic tones
- **Instant generation** — Describe your email and get a polished draft in seconds
- **Streaming mode** — Watch your email being written in real-time
- **Draft saving** — Save up to 20 drafts for later use
- **Multi-provider** — Works with Anthropic, OpenAI, and Gemini

## Usage

1. Select a tone from the dropdown
2. Describe what your email should say
3. Click "Generate Email" or "Generate (Streaming)" for real-time output
4. Copy the result to your clipboard or save it as a draft

## Permissions

- `ai` — Required for AI-powered email generation
- `storage.kv` — Used to persist saved drafts
